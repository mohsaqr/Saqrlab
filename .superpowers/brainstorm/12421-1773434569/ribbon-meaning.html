What Should the Ribbon Encode?

The ribbon width around each vertex's trajectory line — statistical meaning or pure aesthetics?

A. Informational Ribbons

Ribbon width = bootstrap CI or rolling variability. Narrow where the metric is stable, wide where uncertain. Carries real statistical meaning you can interpret.

B. Aesthetic Ribbons

Fixed-width gradient fade around each line. No statistical meaning — purely visual weight. Makes lines distinguishable, gives depth and polish. Simpler to implement.
